GiddyGang

Interview with GiddyGang

Originating from Oslo, GiddyGang is a band deeply entrenched in robust old-school hip-hop beats. The essence of their music perfectly encapsulates a positive vibe, as reflected in the band's name, "giddy." They recently unveiled their latest EP, "Art over Profit."
Dive into an interview with them, witness their uplifting performance in Bergen, and immerse yourself in the infectious feel-good tunes that define their musical prowess!
