Atari

Interview with Atari

Hailing from Bergen, Atari came to life during their studies at the Grieg Academy. Their musical style is often classified as neo-soul, offering a distinctive blend of rock, jazz, indie, and poignant lyrics that sets them apart. The band recently unveiled their debut album, "Sweet and Sour."
